Parts of computers

CPU A CPU is like the brian of the computer. CPU stands for Central Processing Unit. Some main parts inside a CPU are like a Imm Gen and AIU which stands for Arithmetic logic unit. The AIU does the math for you in a computer. Imm Gen stands for Imediate generator. The Imm Gen generates numbers from circuit in the computer.Another part of a computer is the motherboard. A motherboard is the main ciruit in the electronic system. The motherboard allows communication with the crucial electronic components. The motherboard is connected with the CPU and Ram. Ram is how much memory a computer has. The most important parts of a motherboard are a video card slot and IDE and SATA Ports. and many more. A video card slot is a card that connects to the motherboard it also stores your videos on your computers in some cases. A Sata port is a plugin on the side of your computer you plugin your hard drives.
