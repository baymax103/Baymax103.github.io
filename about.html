About

Network Bay was founded on April 13, 2020, as a website where children and adults alike could learn about computer and internet. It is a non-profit organization whose purpose is to serve as a dynamic and interactive learning tool for parents and students.

We have one simple mission: to provide a rich informational place for everyone to learn about Computers. Our purpose is to serve as a dynamic and interactive tool for parents and students to learn about places around the world, or simply to provide virtual tours and videos of various places across the country. We help promote Computer awareness.
